Current Side Project

Data Acquisition for Remote Control Car using RF and LoRa

May 2024 - Present
  • Goal of the project is to show that data can be transferred from a transmitter to a receiver using RF and LoRa.
  • Built the schematics for the transmitter and receiver on KiCAD.
  • Currently making the firmware in C++ to be programmed onto an Arduino for the Transmitter and ESP 32 for the Receiver. Software will be tested on breadboard designs based on the schematics.
  • Data that will be transmitted to the receiver will include: GPS Coordinates, Speed, Rotation, Altitude, and Temperature. Transmitter data will be saved onto an SD Card as well.
  • Will create the Printed Circuit Boards once the Firmware is complete.
  • After the delivery of the PCBs the project will be tested outdoors with the transmitter placed on an RC Car, and the receiver will be held by myself to compare how well RF or LoRa communication can work at long distances.
